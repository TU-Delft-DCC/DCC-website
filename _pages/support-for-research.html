Support for Research

If you have a support question for a digital aspect of your research, please send us an email.

For smaller requests for support, which are estimated to take less than 30 hours, simply email us.

If you need more support, you can request that a Data Manager or Research Software Engineer (RSE) joins your group for up to two days per week, for a period of up to 6 months. Read more and apply here.
